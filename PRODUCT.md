# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Founders, indie hackers, and AI builders who post daily on X and know replies drive growth, but lack time to scan the feed all day. Single-account operators in v1 — not agencies.

## Product Purpose

ReplyPilot surfaces reply-worthy conversations early and drafts voice-matched replies so users ship more replies inside the short engagement window. The wedge is conversation discovery + timing, not commodity voice generation. Success: a high share of generated replies used with no/minor edits, with every post still requiring an explicit human click.

## Brand Personality

Authoritative, literary, precise — a private writing room for X, not a broad SaaS dashboard. Editorial Dark Chrome: charcoal surfaces, Instrument Serif headlines, Inter UI, orange (`#ff4400`) used sparingly for signal and primary action.

## Anti-references

- Stock Neutral / Butter / Gothic Astryx looks
- Fake-precision engagement scores ("92% engagement")
- Auto-publish / bot-composer UX
- Soft card-shadow SaaS chrome and glassmorphism
- Dense "settings dump" modals that ignore the app's editorial hierarchy

## Design Principles

1. **Discovery is the product** — keep source, timing, and opportunity signals sharper than writing chrome.
2. **Human send is sacred** — never imply automation of posting; suggest only.
3. **Three options, real reasons** — decision clarity over option spam or fake scores.
4. **Editorial tool density** — 1px borders, serif titles for page/dialog moments, sans for controls; orange as signal not wash.
5. **Earned familiarity** — standard affordances (tabs, switches, explicit Save) dressed in Dark Chrome, not reinvented widgets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Primary mobile actions ≥44px. Prefer visible focus rings, labeled form controls, and `prefers-reduced-motion` respect. Contrast: body text ≥4.5:1 on charcoal surfaces; muted-foreground (`#a1a1aa`) is the secondary floor.
