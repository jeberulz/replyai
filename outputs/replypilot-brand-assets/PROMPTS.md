# ReplyPilot Brand Asset Prompts

All images use the built-in ImageGen path and the selected creative direction, **The Signal Desk**.

## Positioning exploration

### Signal Desk

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: creative-direction route image
Primary request: a private editorial intelligence desk for live online conversations, expressed as a precise dark instrument rather than a literal office
Scene/backdrop: deep black outer chrome and charcoal work surface with fine timing traces, ranked signal rows, cropped annotations, and one small orange live-window marker
Style/medium: premium editorial product visualization, restrained 3D and graphic realism, tactile matte surfaces, subtle screen grain
Composition/framing: square composition, one clear focal point, asymmetrical crop, no readable copy
Lighting/mood: low-key controlled studio light, calm, precise, trustworthy
Color palette: black, charcoal, warm off-white, sparse `#ff4400`
Constraints: no X logo, no fake metrics, no readable customer data, no literal radar, no people, no text, no watermark
Avoid: sci-fi cockpit, cyberpunk neon, blue-purple gradients, glassmorphism, AI orb, mascot, rocket, speech bubbles
