# ReplyPilot Brand Assets

## Selected creative direction

**The Signal Desk** presents ReplyPilot as a private editorial intelligence instrument for live conversations. The visual system combines the instrument-like precision of the Signal Desk with the timing motif from the Live Window.

The full strategic rationale and visual rules are in [`docs/CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md`](../../docs/CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md).

## Final assets

### `asset-hero-signal-desk.png`

- Layout: image content on the right with dark negative space on the left for deterministic copy.

## Solo-founder production rules

1. Reuse the hero for at least the first 90 days; crop it rather than commissioning a new visual for every post.
2. Let screenshots carry product proof. Place them over black or charcoal fields with one orange timing cue.
3. Use the launch poster only for major announcements. Routine posts should use plain typography or a product screenshot.
4. Keep orange below roughly 10% of each composition.
5. Do not introduce a second illustration style, mascot, gradient language, or photography treatment during launch.
6. Add exact product copy and UI labels deterministically in design tools or code; do not rely on generated microtext.
