# Brand Context

## Brand
- **Name**: ReplyPilot AI
- **Category**: AI social engagement tool (X/Twitter conversation discovery, timing, and reply generation)
- **Description**: Gives you a ranked list of X conversations worth replying to, plus a reply that already sounds like you, so you can ship more replies that land — before the reply window closes.
- **Stage**: Early (demo-mode fallback for missing API keys; monetization model not yet decided)
- **Website**: N/A

## Audience
- **Primary Audience**: Founders, indie hackers, and AI builders who post daily on X and know replies drive growth, but don't have time to scan X all day. Single-account users — not agencies managing multiple accounts (explicitly out of scope for v1).
- **Key Problem**: Finding the right conversation early and adding something no one else said — today that's manual, split across X, ChatGPT, and notes apps, and the best reply window on a viral tweet is usually under two hours.
- **Their Language**: N/A — not yet gathered from reviews/interviews.

## Positioning
- **Differentiation**: Voice-matched tweet generation is already a commodity (Typefully, Tweethunter, Hypefury all do some version of it). The wedge is conversation discovery + timing — finding the right thing to reply to before the window closes, not just writing it well.
- **Competitors**: Typefully, Tweethunter, Hypefury
- **Market Position**: Niche — precision tool for serious repliers, not a mass-market scheduling/writing suite

## Brand Personality
- **Personality Words**: Sharp, direct, confident
- **Tone**: Casual but not playful — leans serious
- **Voice Admires**: N/A

## Values & Mission
- **Core Values**:
  1. Quality over volume — 3 options not 10; north star rewards no/minor-edit usage, not reply count
  2. Earned trust, never borrowed — no fake-precision scores until real data backs them; a human clicks send on every single reply, always
  3. Timing is the product — discovery + timing before the ~2-hour window closes, not just better writing
  4. Built for one person doing the work — not a diluted agency tool
- **Mission**: Help serious X repliers find the right conversation at the right moment and reply with something worth sending — without becoming a slower version of the voice-generation tools that already exist.

## Goals
- **Primary Goal**: Prove the discovery + timing wedge outperforms voice-generation-alone competitors before expanding to adjacent platforms (LinkedIn/Threads/Bluesky) or the browser extension, and before finalizing a monetization model.
- **Key Metrics**: North star — % of generated replies used with no/minor edits, per active user per week. Supporting — week-2 retention; ratio of replies sent to replies that got a response back.
